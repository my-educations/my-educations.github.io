About us



学习大师（StudyMax）由StuMax研究院（StuMax Academy）于2020年12月1日创立。我们是连接学校、老师和学生的教育在线服务（education-oriented online service）平台，提供教育科普、留学资讯、信息咨询和代理申请等服务，通过大数据和人工智能为每一位学生匹配最优的学习方案和教育模式。 无论是本土大学、国外大学、公立大学、私立大学、综合大学还是专科大学，每一个学生都有适合自己的独一无二的学校和人生。我们相信：命运需要自己掌握，教育也要自己决定。 没有绝对的垃圾，只有放错了地方的资源！匹配到合适自己的最好教育，人生才更有希望！早点规划教育，计划人生，成功才会早点到来！我们的座右铭是：与其少付学费，不如上好学校！

Our Vision

To be the market leader in providing one-stop study solutions for students and educators. To add value for our partner institutions and all other stakeholders.

Our Mission

To provide optimal solutions for people seeking to study abroad to receive the most appropriate education based on their affinity. To provide quality solutions for educators to find the students that best match their research interests and needs. To build a strong and credible relationship with partner institutions by recognizing shared values and goals

Our Value

First, do what you can to support the growth and success of your students. Second, over-communication is better than under-communication. Third, find joy in your work. Fourth, don’t just value people but also ensure people know they are valued.

Our Service

Academic Consultation, Student Management Services, Academic Writing Services, etc.

Our Motto & Slogan

It's far better to study in a wonderful school with a fair tuition rather than a fair school with a wonderful tuition.
