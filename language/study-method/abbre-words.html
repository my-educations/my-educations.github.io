英语聊天常用语缩写词的含义你都知道吗？

2021年1月7日


序言


现代的社会，英文字缩写词已普遍运用在每一个人的生活中，除了能够在紧急情况下表达自己的意思，还能在开会或是忙碌的时候大大节省打字时间。一旦你不明白这些英文缩写词真正的意思，那就很容易产生误会和尴尬。


一


AIR - As I remember
AKA - Also Known As
AMA - Ask me anything
ASAP - As soon as possible
AYS - Are you serious?
BIB - Boss is back
BTT - Back to topic
CM - Call me
CSL - Can’t stop laughing
CU - See you
DAFUQ - (What) the *Freak*?
DKDC - Don’t know, don’t care
EOD - End of day/ discussion
F2F - Face to Face
G2CU - Good To See You
GNITE - Good Night
IA8 - I already ate


二


MMB - Message Me Back
N1 - Nice One
N2M - Nothing too much
NOYB - None Of Your Business
O4U - Only for you
OT - Off Topic
OTOH - On The Other Hand
PIP - Peeing In Pants (笑到很夸张)
QT - Cutie
SMH - Shaking My Head
SOAB - Son of a *B*
TBL - Text Back Later
TGIF - Thank God it’s Friday
TIA - Thanks in advance
TTYL - Talk to you later
UW - You’re welcome
W/O - Without


例子


"Lol， wuz gr8 2 c u 2 but omg gtg tty!".
看的一头雾水的聊天简讯，其实真正的意思是：Lol (laughing out loud), wuz (was) gr8 2 c u 2 (great to see you too) but omg (oh my god) gtg (good to go) ttyl (talk to you later)！


最后


如今，大部分的年轻人都广泛使用缩写词来交流，虽然是比较方面和简单，但它也使人们越变越懒惰，同时也渐渐忘记英文生词。
