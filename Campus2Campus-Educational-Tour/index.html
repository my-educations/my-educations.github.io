Campus2Campus Educational Tour - Round 1

 

MalayEducation.com

Intro/介绍

Join the Campus2Campus education tour will take you (students) on an exploratory tour of leading universities in Malaysia. Find out for yourself what each university has to offer before you enrol.

加入Campus2Campus校园教育旅行项目，可以让你（学生）亲身体验马来西亚顶尖学府的探索之旅；并在入学注册之前，便可知晓每一所学校所能提供的教育内容！

This program is to help you (parents/students) in the process of finding the right university, our Campus2Campus initiated the first educational tour of several universities/colleges in Malaysia.

本项目重点帮你（学生和家长）发现合适的入读学校，而且Campus2Campus是我们初次在马来西亚发起的跨学校教育旅行项目！

Furthermore, this education tour enables you (parents/students) to find out more about your options at different levels. To make the most of your visit, you (parents/students) should come up with a list of questions to ask the universities/colleges that you will be visiting during the tour.

此外，本次教育旅行项目可以让你（学生和家长）从不同层次了解更多关于心仪学校的内容。为了不虚此行，你（学生和家长）可以提前列好关于本次所要拜访学校的任何想问的问题！

Aim of the Educational Tour/教育旅行目的

To take you (students) on a conducted tour of selected universities and colleges and build connection with these schools in and around Kuala Lumpur and the Klang Valley.

带领学生参观和拜访目标大学和学院，并与学校建立联系，活动主要在吉隆坡和巴生谷附近一带。

What We Offer/你的收获

At the end of this education tour, you (students) would have come to a decision which university/college suits you best. You will tour the institutions and see for yourself what each institution has to offer. You will no longer need to rely on websites, college brochures, education fairs, or agents.

在本次教育旅行之后，你（学生）将知道哪所学校更适合你。而且，现场接触相关学校后，你会了解每个学校所能提供的教育。你将不再被各种网站、大学宣传册、教育展览会和中介结构忽悠和左右意见！

Core Structure of the Educational Tour/游学项目主要安排

During this tour, you (students) will:

在本次教育旅行中，你将收获：

1) Attend a Welcome Speech/参加欢迎致辞;
2) Talk to Academic Staff/与大学老师交流;
3) Be taken on a familiarisation tour among universities/开启各种大学熟悉之旅;
4) Talk to fellow students from your country/与同胞学长学姐交流;
5) Obtain on-the-spot counselling and if possible, Offer Letter/可以现场咨询，甚至直接获得大学录取;
6) Experience the culture of the host country and lifestyle and living conditions of foreign students/体验马来西亚本地文化，以及外国留学生的生活方式和居住条件;
7) Be given advice on where to apply for part-time work/了解如何申请本地兼职工作;
8) Participants of this programme will also be taken to some interesting sights of the country, do some sightseeing/所有参与本项目的人将游览马来西亚本地的著名景点，开启观光旅游.

Duration of the Educational Tour/游学项目周期

The duration of this educational tour will take around 10 days (From March 1st, 2020 to March 10th, 2020).

本游学项目为期一周，从2020年3月1日至2020年3月10日。

Application Deadline/报名截止日期

Please Submit your application before the deadline (February 10th, 2020).

请务必在截止日期之前提交申请，截止日期为2020年2月10日。

Acceptance Notice/录取通知

The Letter of Acceptance will be issued and sent to all admitted students within one week after application deadline.

录取通知书将在报名截止日期后的一周之内处理，并向所有被录取的学生发送。

Accommodation/住宿

To enable participants (you) of this programme closely experience what student life is like in Malaysia, you will be accommodated at university residential colleges or stay in college hostels. In the event if the hostels are fully occupied then an alternative arrangement is to stay at budget hotels within the city.

为了让本项目的参与者（你）切身体验马来西亚的学生生活，你将被安排在大学宿舍或大学旅馆住宿。如果项目期间，大学旅馆没有多余房间，我们将安排你住在本市的其他经济型酒店。

Transportation/交通

Students (you) of this programme will be transported either by university owned buses or coaches or private chartered buses which are fully air-conditioned.

本项目的主要交通工具是：学校巴士或者校车，或者我们给你安排的私人大巴，所以交通工具都带有空调。

Meals/饮食

Due to the wide variety of cheap Malaysian culinary available, no pre-arranged meals will be provided. However, the participants (you) will be taken to popular halal and non-halal food outlets during meal times.

由于本地马来西亚美食随处可见，好吃又便宜，所以我们将不安排统一就餐。但我们会带你去比较出名的美食店就餐，清真不清真的都有。

Air-Fare/来往机票

Participants (you) of this programme will confirm and purchase their round-trip air-tickets from your own country.

你需要自行购买往返机票（推荐买马来西亚亚航机票，比较实惠）。

Registration Fee/注册费用

The whole registration fee of the educational tour program is 2,800 RM. A non-refundable deposit 500 RM is required once you accept the Educational Tour Offer, the rest 2,300 RM will be paid in the first day of the educational tour. The payment will be processed by WeChat Pay or Alipay.

整个教育旅行项目的费用是2800马币。如果同意接收本项目通知书，需要先支付不可退款的500马币定金；剩下的2300马币费用需要在项目的第一天支付。所有费用支付将通过微信支付或支付宝完成（1马币约等于1.7人民币）。

How to Apply/报名方式

Fill THIS FORM and submit first, we will review the information and check your additional materials to ensure authenticity.

先填好「此表」并提交，稍后我们会审查信息并核对你的额外资料，确保信息真实可靠。

Summary/总结

In short, the Campus2Campus program is an unique opportunity of experiencing wonderful educational tour within your reach and within your budget.

总而言之，本次Campus2Campus项目是一次可以体验教育旅行且难能可贵的机会。触手可及，又不超你预算！
