Campus2Campus Educational Tour

 

MalayEducation.com

Intro/介绍

Join the Campus2Campus education tour will take you on an exploratory tour of leading universities in Malaysia. Find out for yourself what each university has to offer before you enrol.

This program is to help parents/students in the process of finding the right university, Campus2Campus initiated the first educational tour of several universities/colleges in Malaysia.

Furthermore, this education tour enables parents/students to find out more about their options at different levels. To make the most of their visit, parents/students should come up with a list of questions to ask the universities/colleges that you will be visiting during the tour.

For whom/目标群体

1) Undergraduate students
2) Post-graduate students

Aim of Educational Tour/游学目的

To take students on a conducted tour of selected universities and colleges in and around Kuala Lumpur and the Klang Valley.

During this tour, students will:

1) Attend a Welcome Speech;
2) Talk to Academic Staff;
3) Be taken on a familiarisation tour of the university, its campus, etc.
4) Talk to fellow students from your country;
5) Obtain on-the-spot counselling and if possible, Offer Letter;
6) Experience the culture of the host country and lifestyle and living conditions of foreign students; etc.
7) Be given advice on where to apply for part-time work.

Accommodation/住宿

To enable participants of this programme experience what student life is like in Malaysia, they will be accommodated at university residential colleges or stay in college hostels.
In the event if the hostels are fully occupied then an alternative arrangement is to stay at budget hotels within the city.

Transportation/交通

Students will be transported either by university owned buses or coaches or private chartered buses which are fully air-conditioned.

Meals/饮食

Due to the wide variety of Malaysian culinary available, no pre-arranged meals will be provided. However, the participants will be taken to popular
halal and non-halal food outlets during meal times.

Duration of Educational Tour/游学项目周期

The duration of this tour will take around two weeks.

Price/价格

To be announced

Air-Fare/来往机票

Participants of this programme will confirm and purchase their returned air-tickets from their own country.

Sightseeing/旅游观光

Participants of this programme will also be taken to some interesting sights of the country.

What you get/你的收获

At the end of this education tour, you would have come to a decision which university/college suits them best. Your will tour the institutions and see for yourself what each institution has to offer. You will no longer need to rely on websites, college brochures, education fairs, or agents.
