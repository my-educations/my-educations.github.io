Welcome Letter from Campus2Campus Edu Tour Team/来自高校联盟教育游小组的欢迎辞

Jan 23, 2020

 

Campus2Campus Team

To All Prospective Students/致所有的未来学子,

Happy New Year and Welcome to My Education Website. Our vision and mission is to help more and more students choose the right course and institution for their bright future academic study.

We are now into 2020, the Visa-free 2020 entry for tourists from China throughout 2020. All tourists from China come to Malaysia for up to 15 days without a visa now. Furthermore, the China-Malaysia Year of Culture and Tourism 2020 has just been launched. This joint culture-tourism initiative would push bilateral relations between the two nations to a new height!

Take advantage of the 15 day Visa free entry and sign up for the Campus2Campus Edu Tour so you could find for yourself what each institution has to offer. What you see is what you will get!

It is now timely for you to lookout for the best educational institutions to further your studies in Malaysia and take charge of your life in higher education. Campus2Campus Edu Tour provides the pathway for you by taking you on a conducted tour of several institutions of higher learning so you can find and decide for yourself what is in store for you.

Let Campus2Campus Edu Tour shape your future and help fulfil your academic potential.

Cheers,

Campus2Campus Edu Tour Team.
